Growing Season Length

Eastern North America, 2001-2010

Projection: WGS84 (EPSG:4326)

Data Source: MODIS EVI2 (VIPPHEN product)

Time Period: 2001-2010 (decadal mean)

Map Author: Bustanul Zannat

Map

This map shows the length of the growing season (days from spring onset to fall senescence) across eastern North America. Yellow indicates shorter growing seasons (high latitude and elevation), green indicates longer growing seasons (low latitude and elevation).
